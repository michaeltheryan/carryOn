CarryOn is a customer to customer online marketplace that brokers sales of unused carry on luggage space between ticketed passengers on domestic flights in the United States.  

Sellers (ticketed individuals who are registered with the CarryOn website and do not need their free carry on luggage allowance) will be able to search for their flight in our database in order to offer their carry on baggage space for sale.  

Buyers (ticketed individuals who are registered with the CarryOn website and need additional carry on luggage at a cheaper price than the airlines charge) will be able to search for their flight to reveal any carry on space that is currently for sale by a Buyer.  

CarryOne will use a simple forward auction (open-bid) proxy bid system to determine the winner and winning bid, which will include a small fee for CarryOn. All auctions will close one hour prior to check-in time for the flight.  Post-transaction, buyers and sellers will have the option of rating/reviewing each other which will enable the creation of a reputation system.  

CarryOn will be initially accessible through a website, but mobile app rollouts as well as an API are in our roadmap.
